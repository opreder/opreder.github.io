MISSION

Improve and preserve communication between young people and adults because the inequality and power that an adult believes to have over young people, is notoriously seen but not given the importance it deserves. For this reason we offer a strategy based on visual media, in order to raise awareness and achieve the goal of having mutual well-being and good relationships.

VISION

To be an entity of municipal reference, in order to achieve by 2022 be recognized for positively impacting the well-being of relations between adults and youth in the community.
