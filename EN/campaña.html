WHAT WORDS CAUSE

He led a young woman full of energy to keep sitting in a corner, being consumed by despair. Everything was going in a natural direction, a little girl was part of a family, where one day she observed her tired mother, she offers her help in the kitchen, without having any knowledge of this, drops something; her mother, in case of understanding her, decided to recite a few words to her, which were not suitable for this situation. Over the years, he could see his father desperate for a conflict, approaching him to help him solve his problems, but with a not very good attitude, he reluctantly turns to her, telling her that he has no knowledge about what is going on. Childhood, has become a memory that in the end and after the passage of time this sad girl, will be anything but sane. Her adolescence arrived, but each time, her world worsened; in her day to day she was hurt by echoes of the past that rumble in her head; she, is ready to do everything pending, taking into account that at the time, she wanted something to eat, her soda spills, her body shudders and her blood boils, she loses control, she is filled with rage and begins to beat herself on the head while phrases said in the past recite in her; passing the days, in the face of overwhelming situations that do not let her feel good, "What can I do if I can’t do anything?" In despair, he leans against the wall and falls down it, clutching his hair with his hands filled with anger. What would have happened if everything was different? Something better would have happened, for sure.

WHAT THE EYES DON’T SEE

In most cases, people go through periods of their lives that mark them forever, these can be positive situations or not. Usually these periods that remain in us happen in adolescence, time when we are more sensitive and where we have more changes... It is where we experience who we are, our tastes, where we want to go and the people we want to
accompany us on our journey. In turn, is at this age,
where parents and children do not have a
good communication and differences of thoughts make many words or actions that stay with us and make us change who we are... So keep care and measure every gesture or word that you can direct towards another person, we do not know the impact that it may have on her or the blameless mark that you can leave her for her life.

ACHIEVING FREEDOM

Finally comes a day where everything begins to consume us, where we try to move forward and look forward ignoring all those words, all those situations that over the years we saw as insignificant, ignoring the fact that we feel insufficient for society because we are young, because we do not have as broad a knowledge as our parents may have just because we are "older" or because we "have more experience in life".
And that day the foolish words ever heard, try to drag us to prevent us from leaving this void in which we fell, perhaps without realizing it, perhaps being aware of it, in any case, without the necessary willpower, without the support of those who are supposed to be our example, with the stigma of being the "crystal generation", with the looks they judge, It is easy to submerge and let yourself fall into a darkness that was growing with the passing of time and that perhaps the only solution would be to look up and keep fighting for yourself without letting go of that inner light that kept us alive day after day.

CHAOS AND TRANQUILITY

Not always what we show on the outside is what really happens inside us. If you ask anyone around you about her I would tell you that she is a very happy person, who is always positive and happy with that permanent smile on her face, no one I could tell you negative things about her attitude but what really happens is that she inwardly feels married, she is fed up to the point of fading into the tears of sadness she keeps every day; you’ll wonder why, the answer is simpler than you think and you may even find it seen not only in the environment of it but also can pass by you and it is that she always hears words from her parents that assure is to reinforce or correct, "it is your obligation" "You’re a nuisance, change at once" "You really like this, it’s awful" those simple words as they say, They really destroy this girl in the worst way, causing her fear and insecurity. Although it does not prove it she keeps in her external tranquility an internal chaos

RELEASE LOOK

Today despite the constant talks by society about suicide and depression, we do not understand and pay the necessary attention to this problem and its background; we only see the outside saying that a person with a permanent smile on his face is not someone who holds pain and contains tears and even if we see carefully people who show signs of sadness we only share brief and empty talks that are always governed by a protocol, when we must not only ask but act, help and as is commonly said put ourselves in the shoes of the other. Many young people today drown trying to contain and endure the pressure that is put on them by adults, sadly society does not pay the best attention to young people who always call under the heading "the future of the country" leaving them adrift from the injunctions and even blows by their parents who throw with the excuse "it is for a better upbringing and teaching"; we must lend it better
attention and not just stop their actions and throw meaningless words, stop saying they don’t appreciate life or it’s just a cowardly decision.
